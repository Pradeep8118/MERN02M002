Frequently Asked Questions

How does Tability pricing work?

Prices are set per seat and you will be able to buy more seats from within your workspace. Each workspace has a separate billing.

How do I upgrade?

Just sign up for a trial above and you can upgrade your subscription from within your workspace settings once you're in app.

What happens when I reach my user limit?

When your workspace has reached the user limit, you will still be able to use Tability as usual, but cannot add more users. To add more users, you'll need to go to your workspace settings and purchase more seats.

Can I get a refund?

If you are unsatisfied with your experience for any reason, we offer refunds up to 30 days of payment.

What are my payment options?

We accept credit card payments. Contact us if you have special needs around payment.

How many seats can I purchase?

You can buy up to 1,000 seats from within your workspace. Contact us at sales@tability.io if you need more seats for your organisation.
